ENTITY-019 “HOWLER”

Threat Level: HIGH | Containment Status: CONTAINED

Overview

A bipedal nocturnal predator capable of mimicking human voices with extreme accuracy. Primary hunting method: luring victims by calling their names.

Anatomy

Elongated limbs, no visible eyes, highly developed auditory system.
